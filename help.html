Frequently Asked Questions

What is Aura AI?

Aura AI is a premium, context-aware artificial intelligence platform designed for seamless interaction, document analysis, and efficient problem-solving.

Is my data secure?

Yes, Aura AI uses secure MongoDB storage and industry-standard encryption. Your chat history and profile data are protected and only accessible to you.

Can I customize my profile?

Absolutely! Navigate to your Profile section from the sidebar to update your name, bio, location, and upload a custom profile picture which will be stored in our database.
